About Us

Welcome to The Olive Cafe!

Welcome to The Olive Cafe, your cozy retreat in the heart of Waterford! We're passionate about crafting delicious, wholesome breakfasts and hearty lunches that bring our community together. Using locally sourced ingredients, we create meals that feel like home - with flavors that satisfy and quality you can trust.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick coffee or settling in for a leisurely lunch, our friendly team is here to make every visit special. Pair our specialty coffee with fresh pastries, or try one of our signature dishes made with love and care. At The Olive Cafe, we believe in great food, warm connections, and a welcoming space for all.

Drop in today and experience the best of Waterfordss cafe culture!
